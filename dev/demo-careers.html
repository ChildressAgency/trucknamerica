To Apply

  • Complete our online "Qualifying" Employment Application - CLICK HERE
  • After completing our online "Qualifying" Employment Application, it will be reviewed to see if there is a match between your experience & qualifications with our current positions we have open and their requirements. If there is a match, you will be called to have a short phone interview.
  • Should it be determined that we would like to continue to explore the possibility of you joining our team, you will be invited to come in for a face-to-face interview. This interview helps us determine if you are right for Truck'n America, and for you to find out if Truck'n America is right for you. If both of us agree there is a match, there could be additional interviews to determine if there is a good fit with our team.
  • If you are found to be a good fit, there may be additional interviews as well as a possible Drug screening, employment reference check, criminal background and credit check (depending on position). If these check out...
  • You will be offered a job. If you decide to join our team a "start date" will be determined and your 90 Day Probationary will begin.
  • during your initial 90 Day Probationary period you will undergo extensive one-on-one initial training, online training, and you and your Initial Trainer will have the opportunity to determine if Truck'n America is right for you.

Assistant Store Manager

We are looking for a responsible and strong leader to assist the Executive Manager and fill in for them when they're absent. Your work will be critical in ensuring the team meets its goals of efficiency and customer satisfaction.

The role of a Truck'n america Assistant Manger (aka #2) encompasses all functions at our stores. From managing the full retail store, to sales, to installation & service of the products we sell. You should also be apt in organizing and solving problems. Interpersonal and medication skills will be very useful, since you'll often be acting as a point of contact between manager, employees and customers.

Responsibilities (including, but not limited to):

  • Assisting Executive Manager is all store operations, sales installation and service.
  • Sales - communicate with customers and evaluate their needs and specifications.
  • Inventory Control - oversee incoming and outgoing shipments and maintain correct / ideal inventory.
  • Installations - ensure products installed are done so correctly and in an efficient manner.
  • Store / Property / equipment maintenance - make sure the store is inviting, safe and clean.
  • Ensure that schedules, objectives and deadlines are met.
  • Supervise and motivate staff.
  • Secure adherence to company's policies and guidelines.

Requirements (including, but not limited to):

  • Honest, Friendly, trustworthy and Dependable
  • Great Attitude, customer Service Driven
  • Prior Sales Experience a plus
  • An understanding, knowledge and appreciation of the products we sell
  • Knowledge and Understanding of Basic Computer Functions
  • Mechanical Background and Ability to read and follow directions / instructions
  • Capable of standing, lifting and handling equipment / products weighing up to 60 pounds
  • Ability to think critically and find solutions
  • Good Driving record

Sales

  • "Go the extra mile" to drive sales

Installer

We are looking for a self-starter service technician to deliver the desired customer service experience. The goal is to drive service success that improves customer satisfaction, maximizes custom retention and increases profitability.

Responsibilities (including, but not limited to

  • Provide service and customer support
  • Manage all on site installation, repair, maintenance and test tasks
  • Diagnose errors or technical problems and determine proper solutions
  • Produce timely and detailed service reports

  • Operate vehicle in a safe manner and use field automation systems
  • Follow all company's filed procedures and protocols
  • Cooperate with sales / management team and share information across the organization
